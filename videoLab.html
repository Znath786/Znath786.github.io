Recipe 1: Spicy Baby Potatoes
  1. Halve baby potatoes and allow them to steam on medium low heat for 20 minutes.
  2. Slice 1 green onion and 2 red chili peppers. Smash then chop 5 garlic cloves.
  3. Remove the potatoes from the steamer after checking they are cooked. Allow the potato surface to dry.
  4. Heat 20g of oil and add the potates once the oil is hot. Shake the pan to allow browning on all sides.
  5. Push the potatoes to the side of the pan. Add 10g more of oil and fry the garlic and chili
  6. Mix the potatoes with the garlic and chili in the pan. Add spices: 1g cumin powder, 1g chili powder, 1g coriander seed powder
  7. After frying, add the chopped green onions, 10g roasted sesame and 3g salt



Recipe 2: Quick and Easy Donut Holes
  1. Mix 140g of self-rising flour and 190g of any plain yogurt.
  2. Once combined well, transfer to piping bag with round tip.
  3. Preheat oil to around 320-330 degrees Farenheit.
  4. Cook donuts until golden, then remove and place onto a napkin.
  5. Make glaze using: 250g powdered sugar, 75g butter, 3-4 Tbsp milk, 1 tsp vanilla.
  6. Drop donuts into glaze to coat them, then remove and place on rack to allow excess to drip off.



Recipe 3: Hashbrowns
  1. Halve 2 potatoes, then quarter each of the halves (8 pieces each potato).
  2. Boil potatoes.
  3. While the potates are boiling, dice 2 other potatoes.
  4. Mash the boiled potatoes while they are hot.
  5. Add 4 Tbsp potato starch, some salt and pepper, and the chopped potatoes. Mix will to form dough<./li>
  6. Fry hashbrowns in medium heat, flipping once golden on the first side.
